CONCEPT BEHIND "PERCEPTION"

The decision to name our exhibition "Perception came from the idea of us wanting to capture the essence of what makes this art show so special: the way each artist sees and interprets the world in their own style.

"Perception" was chosen because it reflects the core idea that art is not just about what you see; it's about how you see it. The word itself carries this sense of looking, interpreting, and understanding, which aligns perfectly with the diverse range of perspectives our artists bring to the table.
